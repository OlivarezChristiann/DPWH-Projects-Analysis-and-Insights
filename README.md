# 📊 DPWH Project Analysis and Insights Dashboard

A comprehensive data visualization project analyzing the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) project portfolio in the Philippines. This dashboard delivers a multi-dimensional view of infrastructure execution, funding distribution, and regional impact.

---

## 🚀 Overview
This project transforms raw DPWH project data into actionable insights using Power BI. It aims to identify bottlenecks in project execution, analyze budget allocation across programs, and uncover geographical disparities in infrastructure investment.

---

## 📌 Key Metrics
- 💰 **Total Project Cost:** ₱740.31 Billion  
- 📦 **Total Projects:** 12,870  
- ✅ **Completion Rate:** 78.6%  
- 🏛️ **Primary Funding Source:** GAA FY 2023  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 1️⃣ Overview & Project Distribution
Provides a high-level summary of the project portfolio.

- **Project Status Breakdown** – Completed (78.63%) vs. Ongoing & Not Yet Started  
- **Top 10 Most Expensive Projects** – Includes major flood control and bridge projects  
- **Island Group Distribution** – Majority of projects located in Luzon (59.4%)  

---

### 2️⃣ Program, Contractor, and Timeline Analysis
Focuses on execution details and trends.

- **Budget Distribution by Program** – Flood Control (FCI) dominates with 91% of total budget  
- **Contractor Analysis** – ₱162.95B worth of projects tagged as "No Data Available"  
- **Project Timeline Trends** – Strong Q1 spike indicating front-loaded project planning  

---

### 3️⃣ Regional and Funding Insights
Explores geographic and financial distribution.

- **Regional Activity** – Region III has the highest number of projects (2,789)  
- **Average Cost per Region** – MIMAROPA and CARAGA show higher average costs (~₱80M)  
- **Funding Allocation** – Treemap of GAA funding from FY 2021–2025  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- ⚠️ **Sectoral Imbalance:** Flood Control projects dominate the portfolio, limiting allocation to other sectors like Roads and Bridges  
- ❗ **Data Transparency Risk:** 2,014 projects lack contractor information, affecting accountability  
- 🌍 **Geographical Concentration:** Over 50% of projects are concentrated in Luzon, suggesting uneven infrastructure development
